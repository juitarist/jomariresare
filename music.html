Musical Tastes

I grew up on 90's hip-hop, techno, and pop. Then during the 2000's I was really into r&b. During that time, I also really got into VGM after playing so many RPGs with spectacular composers. I sum it up to the melodies and harmonies of the music. This was evident when I discovered Vocaloid music. Almost like video game MIDIs, their melodies were on point and very pleasing to my ear. As it happens, those melodies were easy to make for Japanese composers since it seems, those are natural melodies in their musical culture. After this whole paragraph, I'm just trying to say I love melodies of music.
